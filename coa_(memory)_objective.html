Objective

  • The main objective of studying Control Memory in 8085 is to understand how control signals manage memory and I/O operations. It helps students learn how instructions are executed and how data transfer takes place inside the microprocessor.

  • Another objective is to understand the role of signals like RD̅, WR̅, and IO/M̅ in controlling different operations. By studying this topic, students can clearly understand how the 8085 coordinates its internal and external components.
