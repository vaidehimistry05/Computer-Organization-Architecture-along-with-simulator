Objective

  • The main objective of studying the Control Unit is to understand how a computer system manages and coordinates its internal operations. The Control Unit supervises the execution of instructions by generating control signals and directing data flow between memory, registers, and the Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU).

  • By learning about the Control Unit, students can understand how the instruction cycle works and how timing and sequencing are maintained inside the processor to ensure proper execution of programs.
