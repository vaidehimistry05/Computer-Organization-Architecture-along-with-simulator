Instruction Register(IR)

  • The Instruction Register (IR) is a register in the control unit that stores the instruction currently being executed. It receives the instruction from memory during the fetch phase.
  • The control unit decodes the instruction stored in the IR and generates the required control signals to perform the operation. Thus, the Instruction Register helps in proper execution of instructions.
